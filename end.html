sebuah monolog kecil

Remember how we learned about different languages in school? Well, there's one language that everyone in the world understand No matter who they are. Kindness! think about it kindness is language in which deaf can hear, the blind can see, a paralyzed can move, a mute can speak, and the numb can feel. thought it's very hard to show small acts of kindness to the people you don't know that's why you shouldn't beat yourself up for failing and doing it. the world is full of pain, suffering and negativity like wars, discrimination, political conflict, even someone giving you a bad day at school for no reason. you might wonder "why be kind when there's so much negativity in the world" But that's where humility comes to play. an act of kindness is an act of service where you choose to be good even when the world throws negativity on your way by being the light, not reacting to darkness not letting what they say affect what your intention is, kindness isn't just about others it's about you too, one small act of kindness can change your Consciousness, and the way you think of yourself. remember, in the end your kindness was never because they're unique or special. kindness is simply who you are, it reminds you of who you are

terima kasih telah berkunjung
